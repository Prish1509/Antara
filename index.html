The Distance Problem

An ocean of guilt, a 12-hour gap, and a phone call that always ends with "sab theek hai, beta."

Existing elder-care apps are reactive chatbots. Concierge services cost ₹15,000+ a month. Cousins forget. WhatsApp groups don't refill prescriptions or detect falls. This isn't a notification problem — it's a coordination problem across six different domains, in real time, in a regional language.

What goes wrong every week, in every Indian home with kids abroad:

  • BP, sugar, or thyroid doses missed for days at a stretch
  • UPI scammers targeting elderly with fake delivery OTPs
  • Electricity bills unpaid, then power cut without warning
  • Maid asking parent for "small loans" that never return
  • Doctor follow-up forgotten until a hospital ER visit
  • Silent depression hidden behind "main bilkul theek hoon"
The Solution · Six agents, one orchestrator

Not a chatbot. A team of six AI agents that take real actions — autonomously, in Hindi, while you sleep in another time zone.

01 / Health
Swasthyaस्वास्थ्य

Calls Papa in Hindi to confirm BP medicine. Auto-refills via 1mg before stock runs out. Books Practo follow-ups. Tracks Bluetooth BP & sugar readings. Flags anomalies to the family doctor before they become emergencies.

02 / Finance
Surakshaसुरक्षा

Pays bills before due dates. Watches the bank account for fraud patterns. Intercepts suspicious UPI requests with a verification call. Blocks scam OTP-sharing in real time. The silent guardian.

03 / Companion
Saathसाथ

Daily voice check-in in mother tongue. Sentiment analysis catches loneliness early. Schedules video calls across time zones. Reads news, plays bhajans, remembers family birthdays.

04 / Household
Gharघर

Tracks maid, cook, driver via geofencing. Catches exploitation patterns. Books LPG refills, AC service, pest control through vetted vendors. Orders kirana via Blinkit when stocks run low.

05 / Emergency
Aapatkaalआपातकाल

Detects falls via smartwatch or stillness anomaly. Dials 108 with pre-shared medical history. Alerts NRI and nearest relative simultaneously. Triggers insurance pre-auth. 90 seconds, end to end.

06 / Social
Samajसमाज

Books elder yoga classes. Schedules friend visits. Arranges monthly pandit. Books temple cab. Sends festival greetings to Papa's network on his behalf. Keeps the social fabric warm.

The Orchestrator

The magic isn't six agents. It's the seventh one watching all six.

If Swasthya detects two missed BP doses AND Saath reads low mood AND Ghar shows no maid visit yesterday — the orchestrator doesn't just send an alert. It calls a neighbour, dispatches a Dunzo wellness check, books an urgent home-doctor visit on Practo, and only then briefs the NRI with full context. This kind of cross-domain reasoning is impossible without Agentic AI.
